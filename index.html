Biography

Hume is an aspiring scientist who has designed experiments, performed analyses, created visualizations and given presentations. His education has consisted of programming in Java, R, HTML, CSS, Python and Javascript, and studies in linear algebra, calculus, statistics, and data visualization. When not in school, he practices skills like concentration, thinking on your feet and recognizing opportunity in an Improv group on campus; he works as a Public Speaking Coach to help out his peers with their speeches for class by helping peers help themselves organize their points, practice delivery and find confidence in themselves and their work. He took advantage of the availability of data at the USF Speaking Center (intake forms that record several features about the student and the assignment) and has created a data viz website of that dataset here: https://hdickie.github.io/Speaking-Center/ In his free time, he likes to listen to podcasts, do arts & crafts and lie in the sun. His goals for the future include getting into USF's Master of Analytics program, reading many more books and one day possibly working for government or starting a business.
